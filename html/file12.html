FIFA World Cup 2014 News

The biggest surprise in the story of the FIFA World Cup qualifier — and indeed in the history of international football — was recorded on 1 April 2001, when Australia beat American Samoa 31–0.

This legendary match also brought global attention for Archie Thompson, whose 13-goal haul in a senior-ranked match, which remains the title to this day, is an all-time global record for a single international match.

And beneath the official curtain American Samoa continues its inspiring rise from subsequent recovery from that 1–0 loss into the subject of an acclaimed documentary, “Next Goal Wins”, drawing acclaim the world over.
