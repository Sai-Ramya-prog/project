Of the insert tab, the galleries include items that are designed to coordinate with the overall look of your document. You can use these galleries to insert tables, headers, footers, lists, cover pages, and other document building blocks. When you create pictures, charts, or diagrams, they also coordinate with your current document look. You can assign a change to the formatting of selected text in the document text by choosing a look for the selected text from the Quick Style gallery on the table. You can also form a text directly by using other content on the table. Most content do refer a choice of using the look from the current theme or using a format from the speech director. To change the overall look of your document, choose new Theme elements on the Page Layout tab. To change the looks available in the Quick Style gallery, use the Change Current Quick Style set command. Both the Themes gallery and the Quick Style gallery provide text commands so that you can always restore the look of your document to the original contained in your current template.
